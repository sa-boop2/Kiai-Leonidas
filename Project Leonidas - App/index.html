🛡️ 7 Core Principles to Never Break

1

Never Bulk Above 18% BF

Nutrient partitioning worsens, testosterone drops, estrogen conversion rises, and fat gain accelerates dramatically beyond 18%.

2

Never Cut Below 10% BF

Severe hormonal suppression, energy crash, sleep disruption, and high risk of lean muscle tissue loss occur below 10%.

3

2-Week Maintenance Breaks

Mandatory between every phase. Prevents metabolic adaptation and diet fatigue. Eat at maintenance calories for active recovery.

4

Track Everything

Data beats guessing. Log all nutrition in Eetmeter and workouts in Gymverse. Precision guarantees predictable progression.

5

Weigh Daily, Judge Weekly

Rely strictly on rolling 7-day averages. Ignore daily scale fluctuations caused by sodium, water, and carbohydrate intake.

6

Travel Training Protocol

During travel: 2x full bodyweight sessions minimum per week. Daily protein intake is non-negotiable. Resume gym within 48h of return.

7

End of Phase Review

Log actual ending measurements upon completing any phase. This recalculates future targets intelligently.
